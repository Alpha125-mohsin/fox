About Me

Hi, I'm Jessica Fox — a passionate VTuber artist and character designer with over 4 years of experience in the virtual entertainment industry. I specialize in creating high-quality, expressive 2D and 3D models that capture the unique personality of every client.

From concept sketches to fully rigged Live2D avatars, I guide my clients through every step of the creative process. My work blends traditional anime aesthetics with modern digital art techniques to deliver stunning, stream-ready results.
