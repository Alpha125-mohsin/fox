The story behind the art

Bringing characters to life

Hi, I'm Jessica Fox — a self-taught VTuber artist and character designer with a deep passion for anime aesthetics and digital storytelling. Born from a love of illustration and virtual entertainment, I've spent the last 4+ years crafting expressive, high-quality models that help streamers and creators build their unique online identities.

My journey started with traditional pencil sketches and evolved into mastering complex Live2D rigging, character sheet design, and full-brand asset creation. I believe every character has a soul — and my mission is to capture that soul through every line, color, and expression.

Today, I've collaborated with 50+ clients worldwide, from indie streamers to established agencies, delivering work that blends creativity, precision, and heart.

— Jessica Fox

Journey

My Path

From first sketch to professional artistry — the milestones that shaped my career

2021

First Steps into VTuber Art

Began exploring character design and digital illustration, inspired by the growing VTuber community. Created first commission for a friend's streaming debut.

2022

Live2D Mastery

Dived deep into Live2D Cubism, learning advanced rigging techniques and expression dynamics. Delivered first fully-rigged model to a client.

2023

Expansion & Recognition

Collaborated with 30+ streamers and agencies. Built a reputation for detailed, expressive models and fast turnaround. Featured in community showcases.

2024

Brand & Style Evolution

Developed a signature style blending vibrant anime aesthetics with modern design. Expanded services to include full brand kits and overlay design.

2025

Jessica Fox Studio

Launched official studio — offering end-to-end VTuber creation, from concept to stream-ready assets, serving clients across 12+ countries.
