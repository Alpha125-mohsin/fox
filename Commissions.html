FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before commissioning

How long does a commission usually take?
Timeline varies by package: Starter (3–5 days), Standard (7–10 days), Premium (14–18 days). Expedited delivery is available for a 30% fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept PayPal, Stripe, and bank transfer. A 50% deposit is required upfront, with the remaining 50% due upon final delivery.
Do I get commercial rights to the artwork?
Yes! All packages include full commercial rights (buyout). You can use the artwork for streaming, merchandise, and other commercial purposes without additional fees.
How many revisions do I get?
Starter: 1 revision. Standard: 2 revisions. Premium: Unlimited revisions. Revisions are available at every stage of the process.
What if I need changes after delivery?
Minor adjustments are always welcome within reason. For major changes, a small fee may apply depending on the scope. I want you to be 100% happy with the final product!
Do you offer custom quotes for larger projects?
Absolutely! If you have a project that doesn't fit neatly into these packages — like a full 3D model, complex animation, or multi-character commission — please reach out for a custom quote.
